1. SERVICE ENDPOINTS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Frontend Web App:     http://localhost:4200/
* Backend REST API:     http://localhost:8080/
* H2 Database Console:  http://localhost:8080/h2-console
  - JDBC URL: jdbc:h2:mem:selfcheckout
  - User: sa
  - Password: [empty]

2. LOGIN CREDENTIALS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
You can log in to the app with the following pre-seeded test accounts:

* CUSTOMER (Shopping and scanning items)
  - Username: customer
  - Password: password

* STORE GUARD (Validating checkout receipt QR codes at exit)
  - Username: guard
  - Password: password

* ADMIN (Dashboard, sales analytics, stock & coupon management)
  - Username: admin
  - Password: password

5. STEP-BY-STEP FLOWS FOR TESTING
------------------------------------------------------------------------

--- FLOW A: THE CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE (SCANNING & CHECKOUT) ---
1. Open http://localhost:4200/ in your browser.
2. Log in using the CUSTOMER credentials (customer / password).
3. You will be prompted to start a session. Under "Store QR Identifier", enter:
   STORE-DOWNTOWN-001
   Click "Enter Shopping Mode".
4. You will land on the camera scanner interface.
   - If a camera is connected, you can print/show a barcode to the camera.
   - Alternatively, type the barcode number (e.g., 8901719101037) into the input box below the camera view and click "Add".
5. Repeat to add multiple items to your cart.
6. Click the shopping bag icon or navigate to the "Cart" tab.
7. Under "Apply Promo Coupon", enter WELCOME10 or SUPER30 and click "Apply".
8. Click the checkout button: "Pay ₹... via PhonePe UPI".
9. Since you are in local pre-production/sandbox mode, the browser will redirect you to the secure PhonePe hosted simulation portal.
   - Choose your preferred payment method on the PhonePe test page and complete it.
   - (If you enable full local Mock mode by setting environment variable PHONEPE_MERCHANT_ID=mock, it will instantly simulate checkout and redirect back).
10. PhonePe redirects your browser back to the app status page, which automatically validates status and generates your Exit Receipt containing a cryptographically signed QR Code.

--- FLOW B: THE GUARD EXPERIENCE (EXIT SECURITY VALIDATION) ---
1. Log out from the Customer account and log in as the GUARD (guard / password).
2. Go to the Guard Verification scanner page.
3. Show the receipt QR code generated in FLOW A to the camera, or copy-paste the receipt code string into the input box and click "Verify".
4. The system will securely validate the signed token, showing green approval with itemized checkout details to confirm the customer paid.

--- FLOW C: THE ADMIN EXPERIENCE (BUSINESS MANAGEMENT) ---
1. Log in as the ADMIN (admin / password).
2. Inspect the dashboard displaying gross revenue, branch sales distribution, item sales logs, and system audit logs.
3. Manage products, update stock quantities, create new store branches, or edit promo coupons.
